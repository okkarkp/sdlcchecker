# Architecture precedents (org memory)

Reusable ADR-style precedents — a decision plus its context — that held up across more than
one project, read by `solution-architect` during design. A precedent that only makes sense
with one project's specific constraints belongs in that project's own `docs/decisions/`, not
here; only promote the generalized shape of the decision (the forces, the trade-off, the
outcome) once it has proven reusable.

See `README.md` in this folder for the entry format and the review gate.
